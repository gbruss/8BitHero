● 8-Bit Hero
● Formerly Team B: Garrett Bruss, Avery Brown, Joshua Mariano, Shyheim McNair-mann
● Classic Rhythm Game: Impress your crowd!

● Actuate the notes at the correct time to increase your score and health
● Mistiming notes will result in less points for your score
● Each poorly timed note causes a decrease in health, every 5 well timed notes increases health
● Health starts at 50 and caps at 100
● If you lose all of your health, the game ends, triggering the score screen
● The game is a survival mode, meaning you play until you lose- goal is to get a high score
● Periodically, the tempo will change and the intervals in which notes can spawn are changed
● Periodically, there is a chance that a vision obscuring obstacle will spawn for a short period

● Navigate Menus: Up/down arrows, Enter to select
● Actuate Notes: Left/right/up/down arrows
● Maximize Window: F4
● Close Program: F10
● CHEAT CODE- + 5 to hp: Shift+Space
